NOT YOUR USUAL GEEK.
JourneyPost Team
A third engineer comes on board

Apostolis is not your classic geek. He is an electrical & computer engineer with studies at the National Technical University of Athens, but his interests and way of working make him an unusual new member of the JP team. His main responsibility is the JourneyPost Project. He is interested in network safety protocols and quick and easy accessibility for all. Since he tackles back-end stories, navigation elements, e-commerce concepts and server administration, he makes sure that functionality, efficiency and speed are an indication of the quality of the projects delivered.
